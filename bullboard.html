INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL OF VEDIC STUDIES

 

INVITING APPLICATIONS FOR A 12-YEAR RESIDENTIAL VEDA-VEDANTA COURSE

Applications are invited from parents of boys, of the ages between 10 and 12 years. The candidates must have completed their primary schooling to 5th standard. This is a 12-year residential program, for the study of Veda-Vedanta, in a very congenial and serene environment of the Himalayas, in Tehri Garhwal District of Uttranchal State, India.

 

This program shall suit only for those who want their children to pursue a spiritual path, which leads to the Bliss Absolute, as opposed to the mundane path, that is full of competition and dotted with strife throughout one’s life.

 

This 12-year residential program is absolutely free of any costs to the parents. There shall be no binding of any sort, even though we expect the boys, completing this program, to help the Institution spread the Gospel of the Vedas throughout the world. This program is being initiated under the auspices of the VEDIC FOUNDATION. The information about the organization and the course details are available on our website:

www.oocities.org/vedicfoundation

SCHOLARSHIPS

 

VEDIC FOUNDATION invites applications, from boys under 22 years of age, for the award of two scholarships for the study of:

 

          1. Vedanta

          2. Sanskrit Vyakarna

 

both to the level of Acharya degree. The amount of each scholarship is Rs. 50,000.00 spread over a period of 5 years, to be paid in equal quarterly installments in advance.

 

REQUIREMENTS:

 

1.    The prospective candidates shall have completed their Uttarmadhyama 2nd Khanda with 70% marks in the final examination.

2.    The selected candidates shall be prepared to spend 4 weeks in a year at our Vedic School in Faigul, Garhwal, Uttrakhand.

3.    The payments of the scholarship shall continue as long as satisfactory progress is made in one's studies.

4.    The results of all examinations conducted by the appropriate authority/Board shall be forwarded to the Foundation for perusal.

5.    The failure in any annual examination shall amount to termination of the scholarship payments.

 

The interested individuals may send their applications, giving their complete biodata to:

                                       Sri Radha Kutir,

                                       Sri Radha Ghat Marg, Sisham Jhari,

                                       Muni-Ki-Reti, Rishikesh, 249 201, U.A.
